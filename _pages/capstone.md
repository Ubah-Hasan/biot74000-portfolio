## My Capstone Project
My Capstone project investigates whether Aspergillus niger can serve as a low‑cost, visual indicator of heavy‑metal contamination in soil. Metals such as lead (Pb), nickel (Ni), and zinc (Zn) accumulate in soil and water systems and normally require expensive analytical instruments (ICP‑MS, IC‑OES) for detection. Our project explores whether simple, visible fungal traits — colony diameter, colour, density, and morphology — can reliably reflect metal concentration, offering an accessible screening method for smaller labs or field settings.
